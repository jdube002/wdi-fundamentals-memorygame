Instructions

Test your memory in a fun game for everyone!

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-sui, Pexeso or simply Pairs, is a card game in which allof the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.
